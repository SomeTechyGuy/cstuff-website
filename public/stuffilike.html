Stuff I like (Everything here is free)

DNS, Domains, Or DNS Hosting

  • Cloudflare (Personal favorite but you can't add a free/ddns subdomain to it)
    • dns.he.net/Hurricane Electric DNS (Supports basically all DNS records)
      • Namecheap FreeDNS (best free DNS Hosting as a Cloudflare alternative but only DNS and no extras + it's simple)
        • Hostry Free DNS (Good alternative to Namecheap or Desec)
          • deSEC (Simple, free DNSSEC, but no satying logged in if you keep you tab closed)
            • freedns.afraid.org (Over 30K free domains to make subdomains with, for example here are some valid domains you can make ihatethe.us.to, hisnameisjere.my.to, pihole.hs.vc, and i-wanna-move-to-the.uk.to
              • ipv64.net (German website with free subdomains such as mypublic.dns64.de or iuse.ipv64.de with NS records to use something like dns.he.net)

                  • Web Hosting

                  • Cloudflare Pages (Overall the best except for the 500 builds per month limit)
                    • Gitlab Pages (Also best but no CDN or simplicity)
                      • Github Pages (Good but Cloudflare pages is better)
                        • Vercel (Use if you like Cloudfare pages but don't want the build limit)
