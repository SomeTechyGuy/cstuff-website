DNS, Domains, Or DNS Hosting

  • Cloudflare (Personal favorite but you can't add a free/ddns subdomain to it)
    • dns.he.net/Hurricane Electric DNS (Supports basically all DNS records)
      • Namecheap FreeDNS (best free DNS Hosting as a Cloudflare alternative but only DNS and no extras + it's simple)
        • Hostry (Good alternative to Namecheap or Desec)
          • Desec (Simple, free DNSSEC, but no satying logged in if you keep you tab close
            • freedns.afraid.org (Over 30K free subdomains, for example here are some valid domains you can make ihatethe.us.to, cstuff.my.to, pihole.hs.vc, and i-wanna-move-to-the.uk.to
              • ipv64.net (German website with free subdomains such as mypublic.dns64.de or iuse.ipv64.de with NS records to use something like dns.he.net)
